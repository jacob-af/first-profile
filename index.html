Profile

For the past two decades I have dedicated myself to the restaurant and bar industry, working my way up from a short order cook in a counter service hole in the wall to Beverage Director of an iconic fine dining restaurant. Attention to detail, pursuit of best practice, and an attitude of "figure it out and make it happen" have been central to my success. I am a prize winning cocktail bartender, certified sommelier, and craft beer geek, dedicated to providing a completely satisfying dining experience. In recent years I have been drawn moreto the back office work in restaurants, and have developed spreadsheet tools to assist in inventory, ordering, and costing. I've enjoyed it so much I've started turning my attention to web applications and full stack development.

So far my studies in web development have gotten me familiar with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I am excited to add more capabilites with frameworks like Bootstrap as well as libraries such as jQuery and React. I'm particularly interested in responsive design elements, data structures, and machine learning. Over time this site will shift focus from my previous career as I continue to explore the exciting world of web development. Please enjoy the cocktail pictures I currently have up, and feel free to contact me with any questions
